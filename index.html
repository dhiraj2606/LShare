Privacy Policy

LShare ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

For more detailed information about our data practices, please refer to the detailed Privacy Policy available within the LShare app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (such as name and email address)
  • Profile information you provide
  • User-generated content
  • App usage data necessary for app functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain app functionality
  • To improve user experience
  • To communicate with you regarding your account
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only when required by law or to protect legal rights.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us at sspenterprises@proton.me.

Account Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your LShare account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to: sspenterprises@proton.me
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your registered email address in the message.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Account information (name, email, profile data)
  • User-generated content
  • App usage data associated with your account

Data Retention After Deletion Request

After receiving your request, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted. Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for legal, fraud prevention, or security purposes, after which it will be permanently removed.

If you have any questions, please contact us at sspenterprises@proton.me.
